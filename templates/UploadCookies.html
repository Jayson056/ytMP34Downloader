How to get your cookies in Netscape format:

To get your cookies in the required Netscape format, you can use the Cookies Editor extension for your browser:

  1. Install the Cookies Editor extension for Google Chrome (or your preferred browser).
  2. After installation, click on the extension icon in your browser toolbar.
  3. Navigate to the "Export" tab.
  4. Select "Export as Netscape format".
  5. Copy the exported cookies to your clipboard and paste them in the text area above.

Once you paste the cookies, click "Update Cookies" to save them.
